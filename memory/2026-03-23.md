## Build notes
- Used 4 parallel subagents. 3 succeeded on first run; ai-payment-follow-up timed out and was completed manually.
- Subagent-built pages needed `eslint-disable react/no-unescaped-entities` — subagents used literal quotes/apostrophes instead of `{APOS}` constant pattern used in reference pages (CoachAccountableAIPage, AccountingDocumentAIPage).
- Lesson: when spawning subagents for JSX pages, explicitly instruct them to use `{APOS}`, `{'\u201c'}`, `{'\u201d'}`, `{mdash}` constants instead of literal characters in JSX text content. Or just add the eslint-disable proactively.
- All pages follow the bespoke pattern: inline styles, useFadeIn hook, FadeSection wrapper, gtag tracking, waitlist form POST, proper metadata.
